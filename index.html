What We Are

Designed to remain exclusive, confidential, and always intriguing. Membership is limited — and intentional.

More than a car club, CAMBER is a trusted circle of like-minded people brought together through shared interests, genuine connection, and uncommon access.

Membership Includes
Private Gatherings
Three signature gatherings each year, including a private CAMBER dinner during Cars of 30A at Alys Beach, along with additional member-only experiences throughout the year. Members also receive access to The House, a private lounge for conversation, connection, and hospitality during select events.
Curated Partner Access
Introductions and opportunities through a carefully selected network of partners across aviation, hospitality, marine, watches, motorsports, sporting events, and luxury lifestyle experiences.
The Camber Brief
A quarterly private publication featuring curated stories, opportunities, partner highlights, upcoming experiences, and noteworthy developments from within the community.
First Call on Inventory
Priority access to select vehicles from RS Autohaus and off-market opportunities before they are publicly available.
Private Online Community
A secure members-only platform for communication, introductions, and sharing opportunities with fellow members and select partners.
The Collective Identity
A bespoke welcome package created exclusively for CAMBER members, along with access to limited-run member merchandise.
